"Branding Project PDF"

Project Branding

For this project, I collaborated with three teammates to rebrand an artist's brand. Our goal was to increase her exposure and enhance her online presence. My primary roles were website design, documentation, and research

My Contributions

I looked into different pastel colors pallet, because the client wanted pastel colors for her brand and fonts that I could use for the brad. I made some styles capes with these paster colors and fonts, to show case the client what sort of colors and typography I wanted to use for her brand. I went through different iterations for the styles capes after asking on feedback to the teachers.

I also designed the website for the client. before I started the website design, I looked for minimalistic websites for inspiration. I also looked at other artist webpages to get inspiration on how to lay-out the website. I went through iterations of the website design after asking on feedback to the teachers.

Key Learnings

I learned how to layout your content, not to mix sharp edges with smooth edges and staying away from plain white backgrounds, which make a design look cheap.

I learned also to create various website design so i can ask feedback and could improve them to a nice appealing website.

Outcome

The project received positive feedback from our teachers, particularly for our effective interview strategy and innovative design approach.The client liked the simplicity of the website and the easy navigation.

"Marketing Project PDF"

Project Marketing

For the second Project I got the assignment to make a marketing strategy for the Company hand-picked. They launched a brand-new website for the city of Breda (explorebreda.nl) and they needed to bring traffic to the website. I worked in a group of 4 people to build the marketing strategy for the website

My Contributions

I came with videos ideas to market the website through social media. From the research I gathered that we needed to market the website via social media since that is the preferred source of information for international students.

I shot and directed the funny videos that promote the website and I also color-graded one of the videos to make it look more professional. I went to some iterations on the color grading after asking people for feedback.

Video with no colorgrading

Key Learnings

learned how to use Adobe Premiere Pro to color grade videos and export them in high quality for posting on social media.

Outcome

I believe it went well, the client really liked the videos and they said it was a nice way to approach our target audience. I also feel like I did not do a lot for this learning outcome. I believe I could have done more, but because of health issues I could give my 100

"Development Project PDF"

Project development

For this project, I worked with the same client as in project 2. This time, my role involved development instead of marketing. In pairs, we developed easter eggs for the website. My responsibilities included research, design, and development.

My Contributions

For this project, I designed the page for the mini Wordle game. I did not go through many iterations; I created four different designs, varying only in colour.

Additionally, I created a 3D animation of the Explore Breda logo using Blender. This 3D model was intended for use as a VR object, allowing users to see the 3D logo spin when they scan an image on the website.

Key Learnings

I have learned to use several features of Blender, particularly how to animate within the software. I discovered that it's possible to import SVG illustrations and utilise them as sculptures, which opens up a variety of creative possibilities for integrating detailed vector graphics into 3D animations.

Outcome

I believe the project went quite well overall. I received positive feedback from both teachers and classmates regarding the Easter eggs I showcased.
