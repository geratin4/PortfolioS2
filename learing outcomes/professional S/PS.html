"Marketing Project PDF"

Project Marketing

For the second Project I got the assignment to make a marketing strategy for the Company hand-picked. They launched a brand-new website for the city of Breda (explorebreda.nl) and they needed to bring traffic to the website. I worked in a group of 4 people to build the marketing strategy for the website

My Contributions

For this project, I used Notion as our planning tool, collaborating closely with my group. We organized the project into two sprints, each with a detailed list of tasks and assigned responsibilities. We tracked each task’s progress in Notion, keeping everyone informed of their roles and the project's status.

Despite facing health challenges, I remained committed and continued to contribute my best effort. Even while feeling unwell, I traveled to Breda in rainy, cold weather to shoot the videos, demonstrating my dedication and professional standards.

Project Development

My group partner and I used Notion to organize our workflow into two sprints. Sprint one focused on brainstorming ideas, conducting research, and creating designs for the Easter eggs. This structured approach allowed us to efficiently manage tasks and ensure a smooth progression from conceptualization to execution.
