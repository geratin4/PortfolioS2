In this semester I worked on my personal development and core values.

Workshops

For this project, I collaborated with three teammates to rebrand an artist's brand. Our goal was to increase her exposure and enhance her online presence. My primary roles were website design, documentation, and research

My Contributions

This semester, I actively sought feedback on my designs and projects (see Figure 8). I also kept up with design trends by watching YouTube videos and following influencers on social media who share media design content. This continuous learning approach helps me stay updated with the latest developments in my field.

I participated in a workshop with a unique assignment where I wrote down what I believed my core values were and then asked my classmates for their input on what they thought my core values were. After gathering their feedback, I identified three frequently mentioned values: creativity, honesty, and stylishness. This exercise was enlightening and helped me gain a clearer understanding of my personal values.

Additionally, I completed a personality test from 16Personalities as part of another assignment. The test provided insights into my personality traits, which I compared with those of others in my group (see Figure 10). This comparison allowed me to better understand my strengths and areas for improvement in a group setting.

Outside of school, I am dedicated to furthering my knowledge in development by taking free online courses on platforms like Udemy, focusing on front-end and full-stack development. Additionally, I attend job fairs and career events related to my studies. These activities provide valuable networking opportunities and insights into potential career paths.

Key Learnings

Asking for feedback has provided me with new ideas and perspectives, ensuring I'm on the right track and fulfilling objectives. Through this process, I learned my core values according to my classmates: creativity, honesty, and 'stylishness.

Taking a personality test was enlightening. It helped me understand my strengths and weaknesses, crucial for future group projects. Knowing these aspects allows me to contribute more effectively and focus on areas for improvement.

Additionally, I have been exploring various career paths that align with my studies. This process of discovery has been both exciting and overwhelming, as I am still uncertain about the exact direction I want to pursue. However, this exploration is helping me narrow down my options and find a path that truly resonates with my interests and skills.

Outcome

I believe it went well. I gained valuable knowledge about my study path and potential career opportunities, providing clearer insights into my future direction. This experience boosted my confidence in navigating my career journey. Recognizing my strengths and weaknesses has helped me leverage my problem-solving skills and adaptability, making me more effective in my studies and future career. This self-awareness has guided my personal and professional growth.
